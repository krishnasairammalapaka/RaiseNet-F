How RaiseNet Works

Three steps to fund or get funded — all on-chain, all trustless.

🎯

Create & Deploy

Creators submit their project with milestone-based funding goals. A smart contract is automatically deployed to the blockchain, locking the terms forever.

  • Define funding milestones
  • Set funding goal & deadline
  • Contract auto-deployed
💰

Fund & Back

Investors browse verified projects and fund directly from their wallets. All funds are held in the immutable smart contract — never by us.

  • Connect any Web3 wallet
  • Fund in ETH, USDC, or tokens
  • Receive project NFT receipt
🏆

Verify & Release

As creators hit milestones, DAO members verify proof of work. Funds are automatically released to creators upon successful verification.

  • DAO votes on milestone completion
  • Funds released or refunded automatically
  • Full on-chain audit trail
